Build a production-ready website for West Hills Capital using Next.js and deployable to Vercel. This is NOT an e-commerce site. Do not use checkout, payments, carts, Stripe, Plaid, WooCommerce-style patterns, or automated trade execution. The site is a high-trust capital allocation website for physical gold and silver. Its primary goal is to convert serious visitors into scheduled allocation calls.

Core business model

West Hills Capital helps long-term investors buy physical gold and silver with transparent pricing and disciplined execution. All transactions require verbal confirmation and cleared funds. The website should feel calm, professional, modern, trustworthy, and serious. No hype, no fear marketing, no countdown timers, no “limited supply” language, no flashy gold graphics, no aggressive sales language.
