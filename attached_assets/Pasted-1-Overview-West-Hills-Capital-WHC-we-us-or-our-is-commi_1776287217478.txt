1. Overview

West Hills Capital (“WHC,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients’ information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information in connection with our precious metals transactions and related services.

By interacting with West Hills Capital, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to communicate with clients and facilitate transactions.

A. Personal Information
Name
Email address
Phone number
Mailing or delivery address
B. Transaction Information
Purchase and sale details
Product selections
Payment-related information (e.g., wire reference details)
C. Communication Data
Emails, messages, and correspondence
Telephone calls (which may be recorded for quality assurance, compliance, and dispute resolution)
3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

Facilitate and process transactions
Communicate with clients regarding orders, delivery, and account activity
Coordinate with third-party service providers (e.g., suppliers, carriers, custodians, depositories)
Maintain transaction records
Improve our operations and customer service

We do not use client information for unrelated purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

West Hills Capital does not sell, rent, or trade client information for marketing purposes.

We may share information only as necessary to complete transactions or comply with legal obligations.

Information may be shared with:
Financial institutions (for processing wire transfers)
Precious metals wholesalers and suppliers
Shipping carriers (e.g., FedEx, UPS)
IRA custodians and approved depositories
Service providers assisting with transaction processing or operations
Government or regulatory authorities when required by law

All sharing is limited to what is reasonably necessary to complete the requested transaction.

5. Custodians and Depositories (IRA Transactions)

For IRA-related transactions, client information may be shared with custodians and depositories to facilitate account setup, funding, storage, and reporting.

West Hills Capital does not control the privacy or security practices of custodians or depositories. Clients should review the policies of those third parties directly.

6. Data Security

West Hills Capital uses reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect client information.

However:

No system can guarantee absolute security
Transmission of information (including email) carries inherent risk

Clients should take appropriate precautions when sharing sensitive information.

7. Data Retention

We retain client information only as long as necessary to:

Complete transactions
Maintain business and financial records
Comply with legal, regulatory, and tax obligations
Resolve disputes
8. Your Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for:

Providing accurate and complete information
Keeping contact details up to date
Safeguarding personal communication channels (email, phone, etc.)
