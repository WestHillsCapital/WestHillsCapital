How to add this signature to Gmail

  1. Click once inside the grey box below to focus it, then press Ctrl+A / Cmd+A — or click and drag — to select the entire signature table.
  2. Copy it with Ctrl+C / Cmd+C.
  3. Open Gmail and click the gear icon (top-right) → See all settings.
  4. Go to the General tab and scroll down to Signature.
  5. Click inside the signature text box and paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V).
  6. Scroll to the bottom and click Save Changes.
